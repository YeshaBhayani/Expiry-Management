ABOUT EXPIRIO

Product expiration deals with the shelf life of the products everday as a part of business activity.Shelf life determines product suitability for its usage.Shelf life dates explains the date from which the product can be used and until when it can be used.


How to manage expiry dates?


1. First-Expired-First-Out(FEFO):Choose the ingredients on the basis of expiry dates i.e. first-in-first-out approach.

2. MANAGE THE SUPPLIERS:The simplest way to get longer shelf lives from the ingredients is to recieve fresher ingredients from the suppliers in the first place.


3. SELL SHORT-DATED PRODUCTS:When the expiration date is coming up on the products, take action immediately or else offer discounts to whomever interested.
